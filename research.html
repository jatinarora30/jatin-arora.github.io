Research

Themes I care about (and build toward in projects and writing).

Safe & generalizable autonomy for navigation

My MS(R) thesis focuses on making robotic navigation safer and more generalizable by combining planning, control, and learning with practical evaluation.

Runtime safety under uncertainty

Planning-time guarantees often fail during execution due to perception errors, delayed reactions, and dynamic humans. I’m interested in runtime safety primitives (stop / safe-mode / recovery) and how to formalize “uncertainty-aware” behavior.

Control guarantees in real systems

I work with MPC and estimation pipelines (e.g., EKF localization) and care about bridging control-theoretic guarantees with scalable deployment constraints like latency, fleet health, and safety protocols.

Working areas: Safe RL, planning, and control.
